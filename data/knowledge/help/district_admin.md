# District Administration Guide

Category: district_admin

The District view opens on a cross-constituency rollup: for each
constituency in the district, you'll see ward count, total issue
clusters, total citizen reports, and how many issues have moved to
"actioned" or "resolved" status.

The Implementation Tracker lists every issue that has a generated
recommendation, across one or all constituencies, ranked by priority
score. From here you can update an issue's status as it moves through
the real-world pipeline: new, under review, recommended, actioned, or
resolved — this is what closes the loop between an AI recommendation and
what actually gets built, and it's the record that makes the whole
platform auditable rather than just advisory.

District Administration is the only role that can update implementation
status across constituencies; individual constituency dashboards (MP/MLA)
can view but not change this field.
