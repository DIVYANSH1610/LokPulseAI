# MP Office Dashboard Guide

Category: mp_office

The dashboard opens on Top Urgent Issues — the highest-ranked, ranked by a
transparent Priority Score, not just raw complaint counts. Click any issue
to expand its full reasoning: population affected, distance to the
relevant facility, historical neglect, and the confidence behind the
recommendation.

The Priority Weight Sliders let you adjust how much each factor (report
volume, population, distance, infrastructure gap, years since last
development spend, emergency/flood risk, equity weighting for SC/ST
population share, and cost) contributes to the ranking. Dragging a slider
re-ranks every issue live — this is the single best way to understand why
one project outranks another, and to see how the ranking would change
under different priorities.

The heatmap shows where citizen reports are geographically concentrated.
Denser, brighter areas mean more independent reports clustered in one
place. Each recommendation includes an estimated budget, the matching
government scheme (e.g. PMGSY for roads, ABHIM for health), an expected
beneficiary count, and a confidence score — lower confidence means the
underlying data was thinner, not that the AI is guessing.
