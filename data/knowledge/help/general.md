# LokPulse AI — Platform Overview

Category: general

LokPulse AI is an AI development intelligence platform. It does not just
collect complaints — it turns scattered citizen reports (voice, text,
photo, WhatsApp, SMS) into ranked, evidence-backed development
recommendations for elected officials and administrators.

Every submission passes through eight agents in order: Voice (transcribe),
Translation (normalize to English), Vision (analyze photos), Classification
(category + urgency), Duplicate Detection (merge repeat reports into one
issue cluster, or let a citizen upvote an existing one), Hotspot Detection
(geographic density), Priority Ranking (a transparent, adjustable weighted
score — never a black box), and Recommendation (a grounded, evidence-cited
action item with budget, scheme match, and confidence).

Five roles use the platform: Citizen (submit/browse/track), MP Office (full
constituency dashboard), MLA Office (assembly-segment scoped dashboard),
District Administration (cross-constituency rollup and implementation
tracking), and Panchayat Officer (ward-level detail with ground-truth
annotation, no budget approval).

The language switcher in the top navigation changes the entire interface,
not just submitted content — available in English and Hindi now, with more
Indian languages planned.
