# MLA Office Dashboard Guide

Category: mla_office

The MLA Office view currently reuses the same dashboard as the MP Office —
Top Urgent Issues, live heatmap, and adjustable priority weight sliders —
because MLA constituencies (assembly segments) are smaller subsets of an
MP's Lok Sabha constituency. Full assembly-segment-level scoping (showing
only wards within your specific segment) is planned but requires assembly
boundary data this build hasn't seeded yet — for now, treat the dashboard
as showing the full parent constituency and focus on wards within your
known assembly segment.

Everything else works identically to the MP Office guide: click an issue
to see its full evidence trail, adjust weights to see re-ranking live, and
use the heatmap to spot geographic concentration of citizen reports.
