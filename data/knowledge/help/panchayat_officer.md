# Panchayat Officer Guide

Category: panchayat_officer

Your view is scoped to a single ward at a time — enter or select your
ward to see every issue cluster reported there, full detail, ranked by
priority score, alongside the ward's underlying profile data (population,
literacy rate, distance to nearest PHC/school, flood-prone status).

You can add a ground-truth annotation to any issue cluster — for example,
noting that land has already been allocated for a proposed facility, or
that a road repair is already scheduled through a different channel. This
annotation becomes visible to every other role viewing that same cluster,
so it prevents duplicate planning across levels of government.

Panchayat Officers cannot approve budgets or change an issue's
implementation status — those actions belong to District Administration
and MP/MLA offices respectively. Your role is ground-truth correction and
local detail, which is just as valuable for keeping the platform accurate.
