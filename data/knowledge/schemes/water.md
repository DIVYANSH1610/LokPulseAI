# Jal Jeevan Mission

Category: Water

Jal Jeevan Mission funds functional household tap connections and
village-level water supply infrastructure, including pipeline
extensions to habitations currently relying on hand pumps or distant
sources. Priority is given to water-quality-affected areas (fluoride,
arsenic contamination) and habitations with no existing piped supply.

Typical pipeline extension cost: ₹8-20 lakh per kilometer depending on
terrain and whether a new overhead tank is required. Citizen reports
mentioning dry season water scarcity or long fetch-distances for
drinking water map directly to this scheme's core eligibility.
