# Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)

Category: Agriculture

PMKSY funds irrigation infrastructure — micro-irrigation (drip/sprinkler)
subsidies, farm pond construction, and canal network extension/repair —
aimed at improving water-use efficiency ("per drop, more crop"). Also
covers watershed development in rain-fed areas.

Typical cost for a farm pond serving a cluster of small landholdings:
₹3-6 lakh. Citizen reports mentioning crop damage from irrigation
shortfalls or unreliable canal supply are the primary eligibility
signal for this scheme.
