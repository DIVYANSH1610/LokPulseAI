# Deendayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY)

Category: Electricity

DDUGJY funds rural electrification infrastructure: feeder separation,
last-mile connectivity to unconnected households, and metering
infrastructure. Also covers repair/replacement of damaged transformers
and distribution lines following storm or flood damage, which is
relevant to reports flagging repeated outages after monsoon events.

Typical cost for last-mile connectivity to an unconnected habitation of
100-150 households: ₹15-30 lakh. Repeated citizen reports of outages in
the same ward, especially clustered after flood events, are strong
justification under this scheme's reliability-improvement provisions.
