# Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan

Category: Education

Samagra Shiksha funds new classrooms, school infrastructure upgrades,
and teacher-training resources, integrated across pre-school to senior
secondary levels. Eligible works include new classroom blocks for
overcrowded schools, toilet blocks (especially separate girls' toilets,
tied to retention outcomes), and drinking water infrastructure on
school premises.

A classroom block addition for a two-section overcrowded school
typically costs ₹15-25 lakh. Justification requires current
student-to-classroom ratio and enrollment trend — LokPulse AI's ward
profile literacy_rate field and citizen reports mentioning overcrowding
or dropout support this scheme's eligibility criteria directly.
