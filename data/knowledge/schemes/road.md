# Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)

Category: Road

PMGSY funds all-weather road connectivity to unconnected rural
habitations, and upgrades of existing roads that become impassable
during monsoon (a strong match for citizen reports mentioning seasonal
flooding or waterlogging). Priority order under the scheme favors
habitations with population above 250 in plain areas or above 100 in
hill/tribal/desert areas.

Typical cost: ₹40-60 lakh per kilometer for a new all-weather road,
varying with terrain. Upgrade-only works (repair of an existing but
degraded road) cost substantially less, often ₹8-15 lakh per kilometer.
Reports tagged `urgency_signal: recurring_seasonal` in LokPulse AI are a
strong signal for PMGSY's monsoon-connectivity priority criteria.
