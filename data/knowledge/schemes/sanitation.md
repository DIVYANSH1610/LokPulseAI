# Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) — Phase II

Category: Sanitation

Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) Phase II funds solid and liquid waste
management infrastructure at the village level: community waste
collection points, drainage improvements to prevent stagnant water
(a mosquito-borne disease risk factor), and community toilet complexes
in areas with insufficient household coverage.

Typical cost for a village-level solid waste management unit serving
2,000-5,000 population: ₹10-18 lakh. Reports mentioning garbage
accumulation, open drainage, or waterlogging linked to blocked drains
are strong matches for this scheme.
